About Me

My name is Md Shohidul Haque, and I am passionate about using data to make better business decisions. I hold a Master's in Data Analytics and Information Systems from Texas State University, a postgraduate degree in Data Science and Business Analytics from the McCombs School of Business at the University of Texas at Austin, and a Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering. This mix of technical and business education gives me both the skills to work with complex data and the vision to connect it to strategy.

My interest in data started in 2017 when I was an instructor at the Bangladesh Army's Signal Training Center. I saw how data could improve decision-making and was inspired to learn more. This led me to build strong skills in Python, SQL, machine learning, and tools like Tableau and Power BI. In 2019, I moved to the U.S. to continue my education and start a career in analytics.

I bring technical expertise, discipline from my military background, and a focus on results. I enjoy turning large datasets into clear insights, creating dashboards that make decision-making easier, and applying machine learning to solve real business problems. Above all, I value data integrity, accuracy, and security.

My Approach

My professional journey is defined by a commitment to the entire data lifecycle, starting with understanding core business needs. I collaborate closely with stakeholders to translate their challenges into precise data questions. This foundational step ensures that every analysis, model, or dashboard I create directly addresses a strategic objective.

I am highly proficient in data wrangling and feature engineering, preparing large-scale datasets for analysis to ensure the highest level of accuracy and reliability.
